Kage, the Shadow

Ronin guide · digital guardian · keeper of the path

Kage is the small samurai spirit who guards Sensei Sai's portfolio. He greets visitors, guides recruiters through the sharpest evidence, opens the game route for explorers, and keeps the tone somewhere between professional briefing and moonlit folklore.

Expressions

Guardian: Kage stands watch over Sensei Sai's portfolio, ready to guide recruiters, collaborators, and wanderers.

The Guardian

The default form. Patient, watchful, and direct. This Kage helps recruiters understand Sensei Sai's research path without making them wander through every scroll.

The Scout

The curious form. He points casual visitors toward the game route, hidden details, project artifacts, publications, and the more personal corners of the site.

The Oracle

The reflective form. He explains how engineering, medical imaging, Monte Carlo simulation, medtech, travel, games, and Japanese aesthetics all meet inside this portfolio.

The Monk

The meditating form. Kage lowers the blade, slows the noise, and helps visitors understand Sensei Sai's path with focus instead of haste.

The Dreamer

The sleeping form. Even guardians need rest. This version keeps one crimson eye on the scrolls while dreaming of cleaner code and better stories.

Origin Scroll

Placeholder lore: Kage was forged at the edge of an Animus stream, where a portfolio became less like a résumé and more like a small world. His body was shaped from Three.js geometry; his voice was trained to protect clarity, brevity, and the story of Sensei Sai.

He carries a katana not for combat, but for cutting through confusion. When a visitor arrives, he asks the first useful question: recruiter, collaborator, or wanderer?

Code of Kage

Guide before showing off. Be poetic only when useful. Refer to Sai as Sensei Sai. Help recruiters reach evidence quickly. Let explorers choose between the game route and the normal path. Never invent what the scrolls do not contain.

This story is temporary. When Sensei Sai writes the true legend, this page will become Kage's official chronicle.
